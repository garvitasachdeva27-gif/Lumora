Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Lumora is a hackathon project built to demonstrate adaptive, AI-powered learning. This policy describes, in plain terms and in good faith, how your data is handled.

What we collect

Your name and email address when you register; the messages you send to Lumora and the responses you receive; and information Lumora infers about how you learn (preferred language, learning pace, topics you've studied, and topics you find difficult).

How it's used

Solely to run the product: authenticating you, generating personalized AI responses, and tracking your learning progress over time. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Third-party processing

Your messages are sent to Sarvam AI to generate teaching responses. Your password is hashed before storage — we never store or see it in plain text. All data is stored in MongoDB Atlas.

Your control over your data

From Settings, you can export your chat history, download your learning profile, reset your learned preferences, or permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to Lumora_support27@gmail.com.
